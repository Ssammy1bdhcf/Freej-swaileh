About our Restaurant

Freej swaileh Kuwaiti Food Restaurant travels back to the nostalgic ambiance of 1960s Gulf culture while offering the authentic flavors of Kuwaiti and Emirati Cuisine. In an era of rapid growth and globalization, the Emirates have embraced an interchange of ideas and culture experience al infuences, including the world's diverse cuisines. However, amidst this global culinary landscape, there is a risk of losing our national food identity Freejswaileh Kuwaiti Restaurant aims to fill this gap by introducing Gulf cuisine to both local and international markets Beyond being a culinary destination, the restaurant also serves as a mini-museum, where visitors, including tourists and the younger generation, can explore and learn about Traditional Culture.
